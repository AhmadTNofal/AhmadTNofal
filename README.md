# 💫 About Me

👋 Hi, I’m **Ahmed Hasan**, a **Software Configuration Engineer at [Moduflex Ltd](https://www.moduflex.co.uk/)** and a **First-Class BSc (Hons) Computer Science graduate** from the **University of the West of England (UWE Bristol)**.  

💡 I’m passionate about **AI-driven automation**, **intelligent assistants**, and **software systems that bridge the gap between humans and technology**. I love designing scalable backends, building seamless user experiences, and integrating modern AI into real-world workflows.

🚀 Recently, I led the development of **Joe AI** — a full desktop assistant built with Python, GPT-4o, and PyQt5 that supports natural voice control, document/code generation, and Microsoft Graph automation. I also work on **Epicor CPQ configurator development** and complex 3D/logic configurations at Moduflex.

---

## 🧠 What I Do
- 🧩 **Software Configuration Engineering** – building, maintaining, and integrating Epicor CPQ product configurators  
- 🤖 **AI & Automation Development** – voice-controlled systems, OCR/screen analysis, and GPT-powered task automation  
- 🛠 **Full-Stack & API Engineering** – Python, Django, C#, REST, and Microsoft Graph integrations  
- 🧬 **Data & ML Projects** – fraud detection models, NLP pipelines, and real-time analytics dashboards
